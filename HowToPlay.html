How To Play
  1. Choose a match: The first step is to select a cricket match that you want to participate in. The app will usually show you a list of upcoming matches, and you can choose the one that you want to play.
  2. Create your team: Once you have chosen the match, you will need to create your fantasy team. You will have a virtual budget, and you will need to select players for your team within that budget. You will have to choose players from both teams, and you can select up to a maximum of 11 players.
  3. Select your captain and vice-captain: After selecting your team, you will need to choose your captain and vice-captain. Your captain will get double the points, and your vice-captain will get 1.5 times the points, so choose wisely.
  4. Join a contest: After you have created your team, you can join a contest. The app will show you different contests with different entry fees and prize pools. You can choose the contest that you want to join based on your budget.
  5. Follow the match and track your points: Once the match starts, you can track your points in real-time on the app. Points are awarded based on how well your players perform in the match. You can keep an eye on your ranking in the contest and see if you are in the running for the prize.
  6. Withdraw your winnings: If you win the contest, you can withdraw your winnings from the app.
